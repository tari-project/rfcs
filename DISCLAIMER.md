# Disclaimer

The purpose of the documents in this repository is for information purposes only and may be subject to change 
according to the [Tari Improvement Proposal process].

The documents in this repository are largely the result of community discussions held on #tari-dev on FreeNode IRC, the
Tari [Discord] and the Tari [community forums]. Many include preliminary concepts that are in the process of being
developed by the Tari community. The release of these documents are intended for review and discussion by the blockchain
and cryptocurrency communities regarding the technological merits of the potential system outlined herein, and to better
organize community governance and decision-making.

If you are interested in contributing, please read up on [how to submit a proposal][Tari Improvement Proposal process].

Anyone in the community is welcome to contribute ideas, suggestions or constructive criticism. You can join the
discussion in #tari-dev on Freenode IRC, comment on the [community forums], submit a new [issue] or contribute with
a [pull request].
